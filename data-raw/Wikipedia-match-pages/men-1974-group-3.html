Group 3 of the 1974 FIFA World Cup was contested between 15 and 23 June 1974. This group is played on three cities: Dortmund, Hanover and Düsseldorf.

The pool is composed by Uruguay (Pot 3-South America), Bulgaria (Pot 2-Eastern Europe), Netherlands (Pot 1-Western Europe), and Sweden (Pot 4-Rest of the world).
