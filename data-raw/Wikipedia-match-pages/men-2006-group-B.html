2006 FIFA World Cup Group B

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Play in Group B of the 2006 FIFA World Cup began on 10 June and completed on 20 June 2006. England won the group, and advanced to the second round, along with Sweden. Paraguay and Trinidad & Tobago were eliminated.
