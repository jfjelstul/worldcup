2002 FIFA World Cup Group B

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Group B of the 2002 FIFA World Cup took place between 2 and 12 June 2002. Spain won the group, and advanced to the second round, along with Paraguay. South Africa tied with Paraguay, but the second place was defined over goals scored. Slovenia also failed to advance.
