1974 FIFA World Cup Group 1

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Not to be confused with 1974 FIFA World Cup Group A.

Group 1 of the 1974 FIFA World Cup was contested between 14 and 22 June 1974. This group is played in West Berlin and Hamburg. The pool is composed by the tournament host nation West Germany (Pot 1-Western Europe), with East Germany (Pot 2-Eastern Europe), Chile (Pot 3-South America) and Australia (Pot 4-Rest of the world).
