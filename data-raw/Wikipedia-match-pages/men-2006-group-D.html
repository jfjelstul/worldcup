2006 FIFA World Cup Group D

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Play in Group D of the 2006 FIFA World Cup began on 11 June and completed on 21 June 2006. Portugal won the group, and advanced to the second round, along with Mexico. Angola and Iran failed to advance.
