2002 FIFA World Cup Group C

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Group C of the 2002 FIFA World Cup took place between 3 and 13 June 2002. Brazil won the group, and advanced to the second round, along with Turkey. Costa Rica tied with Turkey, but the second place was defined over goals difference. China PR also failed to advance.
