1954 FIFA World Cup Group 1

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Group 1 of the 1954 FIFA World Cup took place from 16 to 19 June 1954. The group consisted of Brazil, France, Mexico, and Yugoslavia.[1]
